The Settlemates Approach

Features

Everything you need at the table

Settlemates gives mediators and lawyers an instant command of the numbers that drive settlement — without spreadsheets, without guesswork.

⚖️

Midpoints & Secondary Midpoints

Enter plaintiff's demand and a defendant's counter-offer. Settlemates instantly calculates the midpoint between them. It also calculates the secondary midpoints — the midpoints between the midpoints — giving you the full picture of where a deal might land. As each party moves, track the midpoints.

📐

Bracket Display & Analysis

When a party proposes a bracket — for example, "Defendant will move to $100,000 if Plaintiff lowers the demand to $400,000" — Settlemates displays it clearly as a bracket, and calculates the midpoint of that bracket, so you can evaluate the proposal on the spot.

🔄

Reverse Bracket Builder

Have a target midpoint in mind? Work backwards. Settlemates lets you set a desired midpoint and then adjust the defendant's offer or plaintiff's demand to build a bracket that achieves exactly the result you're looking for — a powerful tool for crafting counter-proposals.

📊

Percentage Tracking

Track the percentage increase or decrease between a plaintiff's successive demands or a defendant's successive offers. Understand at a glance how much each side has moved from their starting position to keep negotiations in perspective and your arguments sharp.

📁

CSV Export

Export your full session — demands, offers, midpoints, brackets, and percentages — to a CSV file with one tap. Use the CSV export to save information to your client's file or analyze the numbers.
